Welcome to Mama Osha

Where every meal tells a story of the Kenyan coast. Fresh seafood, rich pilau and warm hospitality right in the heart of Nairobi.

Our Story

Mama Osha was founded in 2010 by Chef Osha Mwangola, who grew up cooking on the Mombasa coast. Her dream was to bring the flavours of home to Nairobi.

Today, EAT EAT serves over 200 guests daily and has won the Best swahili restaurant in Nairobi award three years running.
